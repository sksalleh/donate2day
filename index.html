Our Donate Plan

Serving others is a proof of our belief. Serving is believing.
When it comes to crisis situations, serving victims in a timely and effective way is everything.

Food for a family

Severe flooding has left millions stranded and dozens dead. There are signs of more heavy rain to come which is likely to make the situation worse. People are in need of urgent food, water, sanitation and medical attention as a lot of services have been cut off. We are appealing to provide help and relief to support families in Bangladesh.

Shelter for a family

Millions of people throughout Yemen and Lebanon are experiencing one of the world’s largest humanitarian crisis. Many families are having to go days without eating as they are unable to purchase food due to a crippled economy. Your donations are helping some of the neediest people including orphans, widows and the elderly.

Education for a child

The community, children and teachers at the school have always expressed their desire for a safe and purpose-built school. In the local area there is a lack of adequate buildings to rent which are appropriate and safe. In view of this, our project has aiming to construct a seismic-compliant and fit-for-purpose two-storey school for the benefits.
